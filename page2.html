"Good Boy"

At first I wanted this to just be a funny project but then I thought it would be more interesting for viewers to have something more to look at and question. So this is basically a joke on the work force, more so on thoughs who run it and act like they do so much work when in reality it's the workers breaking their backs for them. I didn't have photoshop at the time so I used a program called Paint Tool Sai. It's a program used more for digital painting but it can be useful for editing as well.
